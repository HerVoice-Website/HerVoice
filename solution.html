Immediate Assistance at Your Fingertips:

Safety is paramount, and our Emergency Button feature ensures that help is always just a tap away. In situations of danger or distress, users can activate the Emergency Button to send an instant alert.

key Feature: GPS location sharing for quick assistance

Secure Evidence Recording:

In the face of abuse or harassment, having secure and reliable evidence can be crucial for legal proceedings. HerVoice's Record Button feature allows users to discreetly record incidents, ensuring that all evidence is captured and stored securely.

Encrypted Storage: Recordings are automatically encrypted and stored within the app, making them tamper-proof and easily accessible for later use. This feature provides peace of mind, knowing that the evidence will be available when needed and protected from unauthorized access.

Key Features:

  1. Discreet activation for recording incidents
  2. Encrypted storage for secure and tamper-proof evidence
  3. Easy access for legal and personal use
  4. Supports voice and audio recording
close
Safe Route Navigation:

Navigating safely is a fundamental need, especially in areas with higher risks of gender-based violence. HerVoice's Map Navigation feature uses advanced AI technology to suggest the safest routes based on real-time data and historical crime statistics.

User Feedback Integration: Our navigation system continuously improves by integrating user feedback, ensuring that the suggested routes are reliable and up-to-date. This dynamic approach helps users avoid unsafe areas and reach their destinations securely.

Collaboration with Authorities: HerVoice works closely with local authorities to monitor and address unsafe areas. By sharing real-time data and user feedback, we help authorities identify high-risk zones and implement measures to enhance safety. This collaboration ensures that our app not only guides users to safer routes but also contributes to making those routes genuinely safer over time.

Key Features:
  1. AI-driven map for safe route suggestions
  2. Real-time data and historical crime statistics
  3. User feedback integration for continuous improvement
  4. Alerts for potentially unsafe areas
  5. Collaboration with local authorities to enhance safety in high-risk areas

The App

HerVoice is an innovative app designed to provide essential tools and resources to combat gender-based violence. With a focus on safety, emergency response, and education, our app empowers users to navigate their daily lives with confidence and security. Whether you need immediate assistance, want to report an incident discreetly, or seek safer routes, HerVoice is here to help.
